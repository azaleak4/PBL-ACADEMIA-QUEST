About us

Academia: Quest is a Trivia Battle game that combines fun and exciting gameplay with educational value. Players can test their knowledge while engaging in a creative and interactive experience.
The game features three main subjects: Bahasa Melayu, Mathematics, and Computer Science. Our goal is to enhance students' learning experiences by making education more enjoyable and immersive.
