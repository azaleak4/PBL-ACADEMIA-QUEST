About us

Academia: Quest is a Trivia Battle game that combines fun and exciting gameplay with educational value.Players can test their knowledge while engaging in a creative and interactive experience.
The game features three main subjects: Bahasa Melayu, Mathematics, and Computer Science.
Our goal is to enhance students' learning experiences by making education more enjoyable and immersive. Much of our gameplay is designed with a pixelised theme. The stages also consists of story mode! Fight entities with your knowledge and become the best student!

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a joyful way of education other than learning on books

Meet our characters

Meet Kylie and Allen, the twin students! They are the main character of this game. Both of their dreams are becoming the top-scoring students, and you'll help them reach it! Players may choose one of them as their character.


Kylie is the older twin. She is caring and matured. Her favourite things to do are to go on adventures while getting better at studies.


Allen is the younger one. He is lively and brave! His favourite things to do is to be happy and fight negative hobbies.
